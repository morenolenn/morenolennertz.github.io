The context

In Brazil, it is common for several federal, state and municipal job positions to be filled through exams that can be taken by any citizen, who can occupy the position according to their performance.

The product

“Quero Simulados” is a mock exams tool for comparing performance among those people who usually study for months or years preparing for an exam. The product was born in the innovation lab of Qconcursos.com, the largest exam questions repertory website in Brazil, when we saw that more than half of the users of the company's main product already used the native (but limited) tool to simulate exams.

First of all:
People are anxious.

Research carried out by PISA (Program for International Student Assessment) show that the anxiety rates among high school students are very high. In Brazil, more than 80% of students consider themselves anxious.

This percentage increases among students who are preparing for civil job position exams.

Pains become features

We conducted interviews with 40 students and we heard that they feel pressured, constantly worry about their performance and say that they try to constantly compare themselves with other students.

Almost all of them say they have little time to study because of their jobs and family life.

After talking to many students, the product
had some crucial pains to take care of:

Anxiety.
Social pressure.
Dispersal.

Home

The home page was designed to balance rational and emotional, sometimes stimulating the student about how close he is to realizing his dream (occupy the job position), and sometimes offering tools to measure that progress.
The best insights from the interviews were turned into features.

Cards

Clear and objective: Job position, salary, vacancies, how many people have already done it and the product price.

The comparative

One of the features most desired by students, became the core of the product.

Badges

Achievements: "First place", "You passed", "Master in mathematics". It was in a backlog to be developed in another quarter.

Product page

The product page goes into the mock exam details, explaining the subjects, number of questions, time for resolution and other rules always faithfully simulating reality.

The experience
happens here.

This is the page that simulates the actual exam.
Here are the main elements that reproduce the real world:
the questions, the answer card, the notes and even the stopwatch.

This interface needs
to bring tranquility
and reliability.

No header, no distractions.
Collapsible sidebar for focus mode.
Optimized answer card.
Space for notes.
Spacing and hierarchy.

Corrected answers.

The card displays captions with their specific colors: green for correct questions, red for wrong questions, dark blue for canceled questions, and white for unanswered questions. The question pills are also links that take the user straight to the specific question.

Ranking page

Here the user has an exam overview and compares his performance with other students, indicating his chances of being aproved in a real exam.

Considerations

The product was tested with students before launch and we received useful feedbacks
that generated some iterations, such as the creation of a ranking by Brazilian state
instead of a single ranking.

For business reasons and restructuring in the company's staff, Quero Simulados no
longer exists, unfortunately.
